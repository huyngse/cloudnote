🌤️ Cloudnote

Welcome to cloudnote — a fast, modern note-taking application that works entirely offline.

No accounts, no syncing — just your browser and your thoughts.

Start Using the App

Features

  • 🌼Create colorful notes: Click the plus button to summon a new pastel note.
  • 📋Paste text & images: Ctrl+V / Cmd+V to turn clipboard stuff into notes.
  • 🖐️Drag & move: Rearrange notes freely like a digital board.
  • ↔️Resize: Adjust note dimensions and angles for better organization.
  • ♻️Rotate: Click the rotate button for new angles and perspectives
  • 🌿Decor mode: Double-click or tap the decor button to lock in a non-editable vibe. Great for quotes & doodles
  • 🔒Lock decor: Lock notes to freeze them like time capsules of aesthetic perfection.
  • 🗑️Delete: Click ✖️ to say goodbye to a note.
  • 💾Local storage: Your Notes are saved in your browser and stay available after refresh.
